Artisanal Television is the online home of director David Franklin.

Clients include The Brooklyn Botanic Garden, Scholastic, McMillan, and Johns Hopkins University. David received a News and Documentary Emmy Award in 2015 for producing and editing "Perilous Journey," an episode of "48 Hours," which aired nationally on CBS. In all David edited over 40 episodes of "48 Hours." Other editing credits include the feature film "The Condemned" (2013), "Britney and Kevin: Chaotic" (2005), and CBS Sunday Morning. David is also an experienced director of Music Videos, and his work has appeared on NBC, VH1, Pitchfork.com, Stereogum.com, MTVu.com, Consequence of Sound, and Guardian.co.uk. As a freelance editor and motion graphics artist, David has done work for Microsoft, Fred Hutch, BGC3, Rock Island Communications, and YPO.org.
